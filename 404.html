Our Story

A Local Favourite
Born from Passion

Bites started with a simple dream — to bring Dharmanagar something truly delicious. We wanted a place where a birthday cake could be ordered with a smile, where a quick hotdog hit the spot, and where a slice of pizza felt like home.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most loved local eateries in town. Every product leaves our kitchen with the same care and flavour that made our first customers fall in love with us.
